Grilled Ham & Cheese Sandwich

A quick, cheap, and easy to make meal!

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Cheese
  • Slice of Ham

How to Make

  1. Spread butter on both sides of both slices of bread.
  2. Assemble the sandwich as follows: Bread, Cheese, Ham, Cheese, Bread.
  3. Add the sandwhich to a medium heat skillet.
  4. Let the sandwich cook till face down side is of desired color.
  5. Then flip the sandwhich and repeat the previous step.
  6. Once both sides are of desired color remove sandwich from skillet and let cool before enjoying!

This is my favorite recipe because it is super quick and easy to make. It also is very cheap making it a great option for a meal when I'm trying to save money.
